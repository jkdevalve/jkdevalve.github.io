Where We Are Headed

We are currently in training at the Missionary Training Center in Missouri. It is a 2 year program with a 3 month linguistic program we will participating in at the end. We are set to graduate December 2018.

Per the description on Ethnos 360' website: "The Missionary Training Center prepares you for career ministry with Ethnos 360. Think about it: If you were given the opportunity today to plant a church in an unreached tribe, would you know where to start? Missionary training teaches you about the complex concepts involved in tribal church planting, such as understanding culture and learning language, so you can effectively communicate cross-culturally. Weâ€™re also vitally concerned about your walk with God, so that in an isolated, high-pressure environment, your relationship with God can survive and even thrive."

We are so excited to begin this training, especially since we've been on the field and realize the incredible need for more training on how to reach tribal people effectively with the gospel.
