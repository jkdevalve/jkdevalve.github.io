Where We Are Headed

We are planning to move to Papua New Guinea in January 2020. When we arrive in country we will be spending the first several months getting oriented to the country as a whole. During this team we will be learning the national language and culture. Once we learn the national language we will begin the process of moving into an "unreached" tribe, and then we will begin the long journey of learning their language and culture and helping them to plant a church of their own. Please pray for us as we begin this monumental task.
