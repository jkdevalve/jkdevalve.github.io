Where We Are Headed

We are excited to say that we have been officially accepted to the New Tribes Missionary Training Center (MTC) for the fall of 2016! This is a 2 year training program.

Per the description on New Tribes' website: "The Missionary Training Center prepares you for career ministry with New Tribes Mission. Think about it: If you were given the opportunity today to plant a church in an unreached tribe, would you know where to start? Missionary training teaches you about the complex concepts involved in tribal church planting, such as understanding culture and learning language, so you can effectively communicate cross-culturally. Weâ€™re also vitally concerned about your walk with God, so that in an isolated, high-pressure environment, your relationship with God can survive and even thrive."

We are so excited to begin this training, especially since we've been on the field and realize the incredible need for more training on how to reach tribal people effectively with the gospel.
