Who We Are

Welcome to our site! We are from Connecticut, born and raised. We know God desires to redeem his fallen creation, and that includes every tribe and nation of the world. We want to be a part of bringing the saving words of God to the most remote people of the world who have no chance of hearing the gospel.

Our Hope

Our hope in creating this page is that you would begin to pray for us. We have become keenly aware of our need for prayer support as we begin to make some large life decisions. We know that God has called us to this, and now we need a team of prayerful supporters to help us reach the world.
