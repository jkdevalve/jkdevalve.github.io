Who We Are

Welcome to our site! We are high school sweethearts from Connecticut! We love the outdoors and everything it entails- hiking and camping. We love learning about other cultures and traveling, and are excited for our upcoming adventure.

Our Hope

We know God desires to redeem his fallen creation, and that includes every tribe and nation of the world. We want to be a part of bringing the saving words of God to the most remote people of the world who have no chance of hearing the gospel.

Purpose

We are aware of our need for prayer support as we begin this journey. Our hope in creating this page is to provide a space where you can learn about who we are and what God is doing through us. We know that God has called us to this, so we ask that as you follow our journey, you would consider becoming part of our team of prayerful supporters to help us reach the world.here
