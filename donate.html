How You Can Support Us

Our Financial Goal

The total cost of the internship is $20,000. We know this is a daunting amount to raise in three months, but we are trusting God to provide for us.
Absolutely any donation you wish to give will help us immensely, but the most important thing we need is PRAYER. Please consider committing to pray for us as we work among the people of Papua New Guinea.

To Donate:

  • Go to https://usa.ntm.org/missionaries/jacob-and-katie-devalve and click "make a gift"
  • Send checks payable to New Tribes Mission, with a note stating the funds are for Jacob and Katie DeValve. Direct checks to:
    New Tribes Mission

    1000 E. First St.

    Sanford, FL 32771

  • Call +1 (866) 547-2460 for electronic donations
